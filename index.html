Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025 · NDPR Compliant

1. Who we are

CallMyOga ("we", "us", "our") is a Nigerian technology service operated under Nigerian law. We can be reached at hello@callmyoga.com.

2. What data we collect

  • Phone number (owner only — used solely for anonymous relay)
  • Vehicle plate number and state of registration
  • Optional car description
  • Time and type of contact events (WhatsApp / call)
  • IP address of scanners (for abuse prevention only)

3. How we use your data

  • To relay anonymous contact between scanner and car owner
  • To send you WhatsApp/call notifications when someone scans your tag
  • To investigate abuse reports
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties
  • We do NOT share your number with scanners under any circumstance

4. NDPR compliance

We comply with Nigeria's Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019. You have the right to: access your data, correct inaccurate data, delete your data, and withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, email hello@callmyoga.com.

5. Data retention

Your data is retained for as long as your tag is active. Upon deactivation request, all personal data is deleted within 30 days.

6. Security

All phone numbers are encrypted at rest. We use industry-standard TLS encryption for all data in transit. We never display your full number publicly.

7. Contact

For privacy concerns: hello@callmyoga.com

Terms of Service

Last updated: January 2025 · Governed by Nigerian Law

1. Service description

CallMyOga provides an anonymous communication relay service linking QR sticker codes to registered vehicle owners. It is a communication aid only — not a guarantee of contact, security, or emergency response.

2. Acceptable use

You may only use CallMyOga for legitimate purposes including:

  • Notifying a car owner their vehicle is blocking access
  • Reporting a flat tyre, hazard, or emergency relating to the vehicle
  • Genuine parking-related communication

3. Prohibited conduct

The following are strictly prohibited and may result in immediate deactivation and referral to law enforcement:

  • Placing a CallMyOga sticker on a vehicle without the owner's consent
  • Using the platform to harass, threaten, or stalk any individual
  • Impersonating a vehicle owner or creating fake registrations
  • Using the scan page to locate or surveil individuals
  • Any use that violates Nigerian law including the Cybercrimes Act 2015

4. Regarding point 3 — sticker misuse (important)

A risk exists that a bad actor could stick a CallMyOga tag on someone else's car without their knowledge, then pretend to be the "owner" when contacted. This would allow them to receive calls/messages meant for the real car owner.

To protect against this: (a) we provide a Report Misuse button on every scan page; (b) we investigate all reports within 24 hours; (c) we cooperate with Nigerian law enforcement for criminal misuse; (d) we recommend owners check their sticker regularly to confirm it is their own registered tag.

5. Limitation of liability

CallMyOga is not liable for: failure to make contact in an emergency; misuse by third parties; losses arising from reliance on the service. Use at your own discretion.

6. Governing law

These terms are governed by the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. Disputes shall be resolved in Nigerian courts.
