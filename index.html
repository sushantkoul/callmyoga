FAQs

Do I need a credit card to start?

No. The 3-month free trial requires no payment details. After 3 months you pay ₦5,000 via Paystack (card, bank transfer or USSD).

What happens after 3 months?

You'll receive WhatsApp reminders at day 75 and day 85. At day 90 your tag is paused — scanners see a "currently unavailable" message. Pay ₦5,000 to reactivate immediately.

How does number masking work?

All contacts go through CallMyOga's relay number via Africa's Talking API. Neither party ever sees the other's real phone number.

What are 112 and 122?

112 is Nigeria's National Emergency number (Police, Fire, Ambulance). 122 is the Federal Road Safety Corps (FRSC). Both are printed on your sticker for emergency situations.

Is my data safe?

Yes. We're NDPR compliant. Your phone number is encrypted and never visible to anyone who scans your tag. We never sell your data.
